Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Imagine: Sally Vegas

FEATURED IN:

Fresh Cranberry That Go Beyond Sause

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350gr) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan
